Rules
The goal of Tic-Tac-Toe is to get three boxes in a row, column, or diagonal. Players will take turns filling in the boxes, and try and give themselves a three in a row. If either player gets three in row the game is over, even if there are more empty boxes. Log in or sign up, and hit New Game to play. Have Fun!
